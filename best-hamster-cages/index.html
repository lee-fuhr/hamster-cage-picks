Our advice: prioritize unbroken floor area over height, and always check bar spacing (most hamsters need no more than half an inch). The Prevue Hendryx Small Animal Home has one of the deepest one-piece bases in its class, letting you pile in six inches or more of paper bedding without spills. That means a happier, less-stressed hamster and fewer cage rearrangements needed. Our pick: Prevue Hendryx Small Animal Home.

Best for owners on a tight budget

If you need a safe, usable cage without spending much, the Prevue Hendryx Chinchilla Manor is your best bet. It’s tall, sturdy, and gives you multiple levels. Our verdict

Choosing a hamster cage comes down to space, budget, and your hamster’s species. For most people, the Prevue Hendryx Small Animal Home hits the sweet spot: roomy, easy to maintain, and built to last.
