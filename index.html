How we choose our picks

We rely on three pillars: long-term owner reviews from forums and retail feedback, published recommendations from reputable hamster care experts, and our own analysis of design flaws that actually cause problems (like dangerous bar spacing, weak latches, and hard-to-clean corners). We never claim to have run timed tests or handled every cage personally. Instead, we track real-world failure points, broken doors, rusted trays, escape routes, reported by owners over months of use. This lets us recommend cages that hold up to daily cleaning and energetic hamsters, not just ones that look good in photos.
